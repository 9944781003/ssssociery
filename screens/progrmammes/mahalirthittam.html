MAHALIR THITTAM

Goal:

Salem Social Service society has been implementing "Pangaru Ammayar Mahler Thittam" of Tamil Nadu Government.

The project aims at the Socio Economic Empowerment of women by the formation of Self Help Groups under MAHALIR THITTAM and Capacity Building of SHGs through various training programmes

MT project has implementing in following blocks:

  1. Attur.
  2. Thalaivasal.
  3. Gangavalli.
  4. Salem Corporation limit.
