Toilet Construction

Aim

"Ensuring the educational rights of the tribal children at Kolathur"

Project Goal:

The overall project goal is to ensure Safe Sanitary Sustainability at SSSS Training Centre.

Activities

  1. The project will use effective for the successful implementation of the project.
  2. Setting up TWO transit schools / Non-formal education centers.
  3. Selection and appointment of project coordinator and teachers.
  4. Student mobilization and admission to transit schools.
  5. Conducting special classes for the school drop outs in the target villages.
  6. Conducting school enrollment campaign.
  7. Staff/Teachers orientation Training.
  8. Workshop on creative teaching methods.
  9. Distribution of Education Materials to the children.
  10. Set up 2 Learning Library
  11. Formation of Local Monitoring Committee.
  12. Conducting parent teachers meeting.
  13. Conducting Free Health and Medical campus.
  14. Monitoring and evaluation.
  15. Staff Review meeting.

Expected outcome and usefulness of the Project:

  1. 70 Tribal children will get opportunity to study.
  2. About 80% of the children would be enrolled in the schools.
  3. About 25% of the girl children would be enrolled in the schools.
  4. About 20% of the tribal girls will complete school education up to 10th standard, 10% will complete higher secondary course and 5% will complete under graduation. (long term goal)
  5. The government will open schools and provide the basic facilities in the near future following special action programmes of the tribals under this project.
