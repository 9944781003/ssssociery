Conferenza Episcoplae Italiana

Project Title : Rural youth collectives for agricultural advancement

Project goal:

To support initiatives aimed at enabling the rural youth with small and marginal holdings to have increased access to resources for sustainable agricultural practices.

Project Objectives:

  1. To increase the capacities of the rural youth with small and marginal land holdings in Salem and Namakkal districts to practice viable organic farming system.
  2. To promote a mechanism for the participation of the rural youth with small and marginal holdings in sustainable agricultural practices.

Project Impact

The rural youth have gained knowledge and application on organic farming and conventional agricultural methods, and about the ways to avoid chemical fertilisers and pesticides.
The rural youth have enhanced their capacities to have access to existing resources available for agricultural development.
Through an equitable approach, the rural youth in the Farmers Collectives have increased access to public information and resources on agriculture.
The focused rural youth have improved their living standard by enhancing the agricultural income sources.
