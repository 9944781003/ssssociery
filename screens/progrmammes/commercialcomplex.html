INCOME GENERATION THROUGH THE CONSTRUCTION OF COMMERCIAL COMPLEX

Goal:

It is a unique venture of Social Service Society for the long term sustainability of Women and Child Empowerment initiatives in my Diocese. The construction of Commercial Complex (Two Storied with a basement floor) has been co-funded by Kolping India, Sisters of St. Peter and Claver and other benefactors. The sustained revenue generated from this building will be utilized to support disadvantaged female children and women in Salem and Namakkal districts and uphold their right to existence, health and education embedded in the millennium development goals.

Project:

"INCOME GENERATION through the Construction of Commercial Complex for Sustainability of various Women and Child Empowerment Projects of Salem Social Service Society, Tamilnadu, India,"

Objectives:

  1. To Protect Girl Children in the districts of Salem and Namakkal.
  2. Prevention of Female Foeticide and Infanticide.
  3. Improve Maternal Health and reduce child mortality.
  4. To provide Education support for Female children.
  5. Eradicate extreme poverty through micro-credit support to women living below poverty line (BPL).
