CARITAS INDIA

Title of the project

Ezhuvom Iyakkam - Cancer Care Campaign in the district of Salem

Impact Statement

Ensuring community driven cancer care for the wellbeing of the people of Salem District

Objectives/Outcomes

  1. Community/ public becomes aware of the causes and consequences of cancer
  2. Changed the mindset of community leads to healthy life
  3. Promotion of eco-friendly land and water initiatives by the community
  4. Caring community take care of the access to (Required) health facilities

Estimated Results

  1. Community led initiatives accessed improved quality of medical support.
  2. Enhanced healthy environment owned by the community
  3. Community influenced policies emerged and practiced
  4. Mortality rate reduced through the good health practices adopted by the community.
  5. Level of nutrition has improved among the children through the consumption of organic foods.
  6. Improved quality of preventive measures led the community become healthier.
  7. Affected patients have balanced emotional state through the family counseling.
