Exposure VisitBack To Gallery

Kolping Group members and CBO members from Attur,Thedayur, Agraharam, Nathakarai, Sesanchavadi, and masilapalayam had an Exposure visit on 17th and 18th of August 2018 to Javvaathu Hills and Chengalpattu Diocese.

In Javvaathu hills we visited the farmer producer groups and farmer producer company. Mr. Mohanalingam, Programme Executive officer, Jamunamathur facilitated the programme.

He explained about group activities, cultivation of organic agricultural products and their marketing methods. In Chengalpattu Disocese we visited the best Kolping groups and their IGP activities.

The household bio-gas plant was one of the successful implementation of CRDS Kolping programme.

Many Kolping members of vadanemmeli village implemented this plant and reduced their monthly expenses. In Vadanemmeli we visited the Federation structure and activities.

Mr. James, kolping coordinator Chengalpattu facilitated the programme.
