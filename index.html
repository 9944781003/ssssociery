  ABOUT

Salem Social Service Society (SSSS) is a non-profit, non-governmental and diocesan voluntary organization registered under Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 and working for the development of the poor, suppressed and oppressed sections of the society. It has been rendering its valuable service in the areas of Animation, Development and Support services since its inception in 1968. It has been working to uplift the marginalized people irrespective of caste, creed and religious discrimination in Salem and Namakkal districts of Tamil Nadu, India.
