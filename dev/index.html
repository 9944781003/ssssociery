SALEM SOCIAL SERVICE SOCIETY (SSSS) is a reputable non-profit organization affiliated with the Roman Catholic Church, registered under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act (Reg. No. ------), and the Foreign Contribution Regulation Act (FCRA No. -------------). Our mission is to promote social justice, human rights, and the well-being of marginalized communities in Salem and beyond through education, health, livelihood, and empowerment projects. Our Director and Contact Person for all projects is Rev. Fr. L. David. At SALEM SOCIAL SERVICE SOCIETY, we are committed to working with integrity, compassion, and dedication to make a positive impact on the communities we serve.
Salem Social Service Society's mission is to create a society based on love, fraternity, equity, and dignity, free from discrimination, injustice, and slavery. We empower marginalized communities with education, skills training, and livelihood opportunities while advocating for social justice and human rights. We strive to provide access to basic necessities and create a society where every person is respected and valued, regardless of their background or status. Our commitment to integrity and compassion drives us to work towards a better future for all.
Our organization's mission is to build a desired society by promoting developmental activities, rights-based interventions, dignity, education, awareness, group formation, and networking. We aim to empower individuals and communities to achieve their full potential and create an inclusive, equitable, and just society for all. Our vision is to build a society where every person can live with dignity, free from discrimination, poverty, and injustice, with access to basic necessities and the ability to exercise their rights.

Our core values

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Justice

Our organization advocates for vulnerable and voiceless groups to claim and enjoy their constitutional and human rights. We believe in promoting legal interventions when their rights are violated, violence is enforced, and injustice is enacted. We also lobby on their behalf to protect and promote their rights and welfare during the policy-making processes of central and state governments. Our commitment to social justice and human rights drives us to work towards a society where every person is treated with dignity and respect, regardless of their background or status.

Equality

Our organization is dedicated to promoting equality and dignity for voiceless and vulnerable groups, including Dalits, tribals, women, children, transgender individuals, people with disabilities, and those who have been abandoned by their families. We believe in ensuring that all resources, opportunities, and justice are shared fairly, so that these marginalized communities can thrive and live with dignity. Through our efforts, we aim to create a society where every person is treated with respect and has equal access to opportunities, resources, and justice.

Solidarity

Our organization values unity in diversity and believes in upholding the right to freedom of expression, promoting harmony in social life, and respecting the pluralistic culture and diverse beliefs of people with varying cultural practices. We recognize and celebrate the differences that make our society rich and diverse, and strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone is valued and respected.

Our Operational areas

Advocating for vulnerable groups' rights and promoting social justice

Our organization is dedicated to advocating for the rights of marginalized and vulnerable communities. We believe that all people have the right to enjoy their constitutional and human rights without fear of violence, discrimination, or injustice. To this end, we engage in legal interventions to defend their rights and lobby on their behalf during policy-making processes. We are committed to promoting social justice and human rights, and we strive to create a society where every individual is treated with dignity and respect, regardless of their social or economic status.

We believe that our work is critical in creating a society that upholds the principles of equality and justice. Through our advocacy efforts, we seek to empower voiceless and vulnerable groups and provide them with a platform to assert their rights. We are committed to promoting legal frameworks that protect their rights and ensure that their welfare is prioritized in policy-making processes. Our organization recognizes that the journey towards achieving social justice and human rights for all is ongoing, and we remain steadfast in our commitment to this cause.

SDG Implementation

Advocating for vulnerable groups' rights and promoting social justice

SALEM SOCIAL SERVICE SOCIETY is dedicated to achieving the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which include eradicating poverty, providing access to education for all children, reducing infant and maternal mortality rates, combating diseases such as HIV/AIDS and tuberculosis, promoting renewable energy, and addressing climate change. The organization works tirelessly to make a positive impact on these important issues and improve the lives of those in the communities it serves
