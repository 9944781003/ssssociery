What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Children

  • Reduce infant mortality rate through proper vaccination, pre-natal and antenatal care services and reduce underweight children through referral and convergent efforts. (Linking with local ICDS (Anganwadi), Health Workers, Primary/Sub-Health Centers, local Primary School and available Govt. Schemes)
  • Ensure 4 fundamental rights of Children
  • School enrolment and compulsory education
  • Conduct bridge courses for drop outs and bring them into the mainstream of schooling through readmission.
  • Extra coaching for the slow learners through tuition centers
  • Trainings on personal hygiene and life skill enhancing
  • Motivational Trainings
  • Counseling for affected children and drop outs.
  • Trainings on menstrual hygiene for adolescent girls
  • Bridge courses for the street children and support their schooling.
  • Rescue child workers and support their schooling
  • Organizing Health Camps for children
  • Educational support through sponsorship for orphan and semi orphan children
  • Providing artificial limbs and necessary equipments for children with different ability
  • Medical supports for the hyperactivity students to bring them into normal life and encourage their schooling and career advancement
  • Ensure comfortable learning in local school through infrastructure development works
  • Enforce and practice children protection policy in each school and institutions/projects working with children
  • Ensure classroom violence free school days.
  • Organize and celebrate sports and cultural days
  • Organize and strengthen active Children Parliaments in schools
  • Form and strengthen Parents and Teachers Association and School Management Committee
  • Ensure child abuse free community
  • Ensure hygienic school environment with safe drinking water, clean environment, and hygienic toilet with enough supply of water.
  • Ensure child friendly homes with domestic work free and child labour free childhood

Youth

  • Group formation to improve solidarity, consolidating their manpower and then lead them to take up voluntary activities for the betterment of their own and neighbor community.
  • Employable Skill improvement training to win better livelihood and deliver from the control of upper caste
  • Career advancement courses and information dissemination for higher studies
  • Premarital counseling to improve the understanding in the family and improving the skills to dissolve domestic quarrels.
  • De-addiction and counseling for give-up narcotic drugs using and training to keep better manpower.
  • Trainings on safer sex practices to prevent the youth from HIV/AIDS and other sexually transmitted diseases.
  • Motivational trainings to encourage the holistic development of youths
  • Trainings on human rights like Human rights introduction, consumer rights, Panchayat Raj Institution, Voter awareness, democracy institutions, Human right institutions, Constitutional rights.
  • Trainings on SWOT analysis, SMART, Time management, Problem dissolving skills, interventions during human right violation incidents, introduction of disaster management, risk assessment and management.
  • A sport meets.

Women

  • Resist the feminization of poverty
  • Self-help group formation and strengthening micro-finance activities
  • Employable skill development courses lead to self-reliance.
  • Encourage to start and run micro-enterprises
  • Ensure equal access and control over available resources and create supportive institutional structure for women
  • Create sufficient space for women to raise voice within the community
  • Convergence with bank, Government Departments, Government schemes
  • Information dissemination for needful helpline services, higher education, technical skill improvement, Government schemes, etc.
  • Save the girl children and remove female infanticide practices
  • Ensure gender equity and promote in daily life
  • Interventions against domestic violence, harassment, woman right violation, crime enforced against women and girl children.
  • Property rights through encourage the parental and bridal families provide entitlements for female members
  • Food security.
  • Reduce Mother Mortality Rate through ante-natal and post natal care education and services
  • Networking women groups and federation formation
  • Promote women participation in decision making process, Panchayat Raj Institutions, Policy making etc.
  • Increase women to make use digital platform and social media for their empowerment and development
  • Promote reproductive health rights like marriage on or after 21 years, right to choose her bride, right to spacing the pregnancy and giving child birth, Right to accept or deny the use of contraceptive devices through awareness generation, introduce better practices etc.
  • Deliver young girls from anemia and below normal weight problems
  • Awareness generation and health trainings on HIV/AIDS preventive measures, safer sex and condom use

Transgender

  • Reunite with family if a transgender person was abundant
  • Rescue from begging/sex work/illegal activities and rehabilitate them through linking with SMILE Scheme (Support for Marginalized Individuals for livelihood and Enterprise)
  • Awareness generation on the rights of transgender persons
  • Education support for them
  • Employable Vocational skill improvement courses intend to stand on their own foot
  • Interventions to deliver transgender if they trapped in risky situation
  • Social justice for transgender persons facing discrimination in education, employment and access to public amenities.
  • Group formation among transgender
  • Information dissemination about available schemes of welfare measures for Transgender persons
