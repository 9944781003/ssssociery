From Director's Desk

Pope Francis, In His Apostolic Exhortation "Rejoice and Be Glad" narrates that a person's perfection is measured not by the information or knowledge they possess, but by the depth of their charity. Wealth ensures nothing, Indeed, once we think we are rich, we can become so self-satisfied that we leave no room for God's word, for the love of our brothers and sisters. Love and concern is missing in this world and people are living an unsecured life. Salem Social Service Society has been showing love and concern to the poor, downtrodden, neglected, abandoned and the needy for the past 50 years through various sustainable Development Goals. The interventions of Salem Social Service Society has helped the rural poor to get organized better fight for their rights.

At the outset SSSS in 1968, charity based works being carried out, and then relief and rehabilitation programs were promoted concurrently .At present we work with the targeted communities by the essence of the Sustainable Development Goals of overwhelming poverty, achieving compulsory primary education ,empowering women and reducing child morbidity and mortality, protecting the environment and building .

Small is beautiful, Big is no more beautiful in the eyes of God. I take this opportunity to appreciate and thank Most.Rt.Rev.Dr.S.Singaroyan D.D, the president of Salem Social Service Society for His guidance and support and all the previous Directors and Staffs who reached out the poor and downtrodden to render their dedicated service and I sincerely congratulate the present Board members and Staffs and coordinators .Let us rejoice and be glad always by spreading love concern wherever we are and by reaching the unreached.

With love and peace

Fr.M.Rajamanickam
